Department of Health and Human Services  

Health Security

Goal Leaders:

Stephen Redd, Director, Office of Public Health Preparedness and Response, CDC
Rebecca Martin, Director, Center for Global Health, CDC
Jack Herrmann, Deputy Director of the Office of Policy and Planning, ASPR

Goal Statement:

Increase capacity to prevent health threats originating abroad from impacting the United States. By September 30, 2019, HHS will contribute to increasing the surveillance, workforce, emergency management, and laboratory capacity of 17 partner countries.

Goal Action Plan & Progress Update:

FY 2018, Quarter 1.

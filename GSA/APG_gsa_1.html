General Services Administration  

Expansion of Centralized Services

Goal Leaders:

Alan Thomas, FAS Commissioner
Allison Brigati, Deputy Administrator

Goal Statement: GSA will develop and expand common solutions and service offerings for mission-support services, driving more efficient and effective processes that help agencies maximize mission delivery. Specifically, to eliminate redundancies and reduce government-wide fleet costs, GSA's Fleet program will partner with agencies to study their vehicle inventories to identify savings and efficiency opportunities, reviewing 25,000 vehicles annually in FY18 and FY19 and consolidating 500 and 5,000 vehicles, respectively, pending study results.

Goal Action Plan & Progress Update:

FY 2018, Quarter 1

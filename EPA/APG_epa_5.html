Environmental Protection Agency  

Increase Environmental Law Compliance Rate

Goal Leader: Larry Starfield, Principal Deputy AA, OECA

Goal Statement: Through September 30, 2019, EPA will increase compliance by reducing the percentage of Clean Water Act (CWA) National Pollutant Discharge Elimination System (NPDES) permittees in significant noncompliance with their permit limits to 21% from a baseline of 24%.

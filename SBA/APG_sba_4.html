Small Business Administration  

Reduce Loan Processing Time for Disaster Survivors

Goal Leader: James Rivera, Associate Administrator, Office of Disaster Assistance

Goal Statement: Increase the speed of loan application processing for survivors recovering from disasters. By September 30, 2019, increase the average number of loan applications processed from three to six applications per loan specialist per day.

FY 2018, Quarter 1

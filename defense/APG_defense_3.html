Reduce Regulatory Burden

Goal Leader: Mr. John J Gibson, DoD Chief Management Officer

Goal Statement: Reduce Regulatory Burden by eliminating unnecessary Federal Rules (E.O. 13771) by September 30, 2019.

Implementation Action Plans will be available with the next Quarterly performance update.

This site is also protected by an SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) certificate that’s been signed by the U.S. government. The https:// means all transmitted data is encrypted — in other words, any information or browsing history that you provide is transmitted securely.

Goal Statement

Build and maintain more modern, secure, and resilient information technology (IT) to enhance mission delivery and productivity.


The Challenge

Citizens need better quality digital services and confidence the Federal Government will safeguard their information. The Executive Branch will enhance mission delivery and productivity by building and maintaining more modern, secure, and resilient IT – at the same time reducing overall IT cost, driving stronger value from taxpayer dollars, and increasing citizen engagement and satisfaction with the services we provide.
